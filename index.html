Welcome to the Missouri Stream Team!

Missouri has 110,000 miles of streams that provide recreation, drinking water and serenity for ourselves and our children, but they need your help. Stream Teams are made up of people with an interest in, and passion for, Missouri streams. If you are already a Stream Team, this website will provide a wealth of information on organizing your Team, participating in a variety of activities, and communicating with other Stream Teams all across the Show-Me state and beyond. If you are thinking about forming a Stream Team, we have information on that, too. Exploring the Stream Team website will show you how citizens have adopted a stream, volunteered their own time and effort to improve it, and have banded together with other Stream Teams to help improve Missouriâ€™s streams.

Start your team here! If you love swimming, fishing, or just enjoying your local rivers, consider starting a Stream Team. We can never have too much help out there!

If your stream team recently made our rivers a better place, tell us about it here! That way we can track the progess of our teams in their mission to clean and protect our rivers.

Donate

The fast-paced society we all live in can make it difficult to find the time to help conserve, protect, and restore Missouri's rivers and streams. There is a way you can help. Take just ten minutes a year to send in a tax deductible contribution.
